1. Your First 3 Services (Tight + Intentional)

These are chosen based on:
	•	High demand
	•	Fast to learn
	•	Good margins
	•	Natural upsell path into your stencil idea

⸻

Service 1 — Concrete Refresh (Bread & Butter)

What it is:
	•	Clean / pressure wash
	•	Minor crack repair
	•	Concrete stain or solid-color sealer
	•	Matte protective topcoat

Why it matters:
	•	Every house with concrete is a lead
	•	Fast turnaround (1–2 days)
	•	Easy sell: “make it look new again”

Price:

👉 $3–6/sq ft

Your positioning:

“Restore your concrete without replacing it”

⸻

Service 2 — Decorative Concrete Coating (Core Offering)

What it is:
	•	Mechanical prep (grind)
	•	Pigmented coating system
	•	Optional light texture
	•	Polyaspartic topcoat (your differentiator)

Why it matters:
	•	This is your real business
	•	Durable, premium, solves real problems
	•	Competes with epoxy but looks better outdoors

Price:

👉 $6–10/sq ft

Your positioning:

“A durable, modern finish designed for outdoor living”

⸻

Service 3 — Custom Designed Concrete (Stencil Upsell)

What it is:
	•	Everything from Service 2
	•	Plus stencil layer (vinyl or reusable)
	•	Multi-tone design
	•	Premium topcoat system

Why it matters:
	•	Differentiation
	•	Higher margins
	•	Portfolio builder

Price:

👉 $10–18/sq ft

Your positioning:

“Custom-designed concrete that looks like stone or tile”
